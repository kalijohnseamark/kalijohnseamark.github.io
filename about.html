Kali John Seamark — Author

Writing stories that refuse to stay in one lane — drawn from the grit of real work, real life, and the deeper questions of what we believe and why.

Kali John Seamark works in construction and writes literary fiction and epic fantasy. His stories draw from the grit and complexity of working-class experience, bringing fresh perspectives to contemporary literature.

He is currently completing his debut novel — an epic fantasy following dual protagonists across three unique cultures set in an expansive, immersive world. The work explores themes of culture, spirituality, and the pursuit of objective truth.

When not on a job site or at his desk, Kali trains Brazilian jiu-jitsu.
