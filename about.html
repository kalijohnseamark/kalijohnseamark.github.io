About

Kali John works in construction and writes literary fiction and epic fantasy. His stories draw from the grit and complexity of working-class experience, bringing fresh perspectives to contemporary literature.

When not on a job site or at his desk, Kali trains Brazilian jiu-jitsu. He is currently finishing his debut novel, an epic fantasy following three unique cultures set in an expansive and immersive world. Stay tuned!
